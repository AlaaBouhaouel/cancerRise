What is CancerRise?

CancerRise is a secure, compassionate platform built specifically for breast cancer patients. From diagnosis to recovery, we provide the tools, data, and support you need to feel empowered on your healing journey — at any age, on any device.

Everything You Need, In One Place

Designed with care for patients of all ages, CancerRise brings together your medical history, daily wellbeing tools, and private hospital access.

Electronic Health Card

Your exclusive digital card grants access to private, specialized services at partner hospitals — no paperwork, no delays, instant recognition.

Full Medical History

Access your complete cancer record: diagnoses, operations, treatments, progress notes, and lab results — all securely in one place.

Cancer Progress Tracking

Visualize how your cancer is responding to treatment with clear charts tracking tumor size changes and medical milestones over time.

AI Chatbot Support

Our compassionate AI companion is available 24/7 to answer questions, ease anxiety, and guide you through every step of your recovery.

Daily Hydration Tracker

Monitor your daily water intake — an essential part of recovery and overall wellbeing during and after cancer treatment.

Mood & Feelings Journal

Log your emotional state each day and track your mental wellness alongside your physical progress — because healing is whole-person.

Your CancerRise Card

Your Key to Priority Hospital Care

Every CancerRise member receives a personalized electronic card — your secure passport to priority, private services at partner hospitals and clinics.

  • Instant access to your complete medical history at any partner facility
  • Skip standard queues with priority specialist appointments
  • Secure, encrypted data sharing between your care team
  • Automatic updates when new treatments or labs are added
  • Works across all partner hospitals and clinics nationwide

Designed for Everyone

We believe every patient deserves a platform that truly works for them. CancerRise is built from the ground up with accessibility at its heart.

Large, Clear Text

Easy-to-read typography with generous spacing suitable for all ages and vision levels.

Screen Reader Ready

Full ARIA support for visually impaired patients using assistive screen readers.

Large Touch Targets

Buttons and links sized for easy, confident tapping on any mobile device.

Works Everywhere

Fully responsive — phone, tablet, or desktop. Your data, always at your fingertips.

Your Privacy is Sacred

Your medical data is deeply personal. We protect it with the highest standards of security and treat it with the respect it deserves.

End-to-End Encryption

All your medical data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Only you and your authorized care team can ever access it.

HIPAA Compliant

CancerRise meets the highest medical privacy standards, including full HIPAA compliance for all patient data handling.

You Control Your Data

You decide who sees your information. Revoke access at any time, no questions asked — your data, your rules, always.
